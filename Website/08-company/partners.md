## Partner tiers

### Authorized Reseller
**Requirements:** 1 certified sales rep · ₹5L annual commitment  
**Benefits:** 20% revenue share · Partner Portal access · Basic marketing kit  
**Territory:** Non-exclusive, city-level

### Silver Partner
**Requirements:** 2 certified reps + 1 technical · ₹15L annual · 5+ active customers  
**Benefits:** 25% revenue share · Lead sharing · Co-branded events · Priority support

**Benefits:** 30% revenue share · Exclusive state/zone territory · Market Development Funds · Featured in partner directory

**Requirements:** Multi-state presence · ₹1Cr+ annual · Implementation capability  
**Benefits:** Custom economics · Product roadmap input · Joint PR · Executive sponsorship  
**Territory:** National or multi-region exclusive

## FAQ

**What's the minimum investment?**  
No inventory or license purchase required. Your investment is time for certification and sales effort.

**How are territories assigned?**  
Based on your tier level, regional demand, and existing partner coverage. We actively avoid channel conflict.

**Can I partner if I already represent another EHR?**  
Generally no — we require non-compete for active deals in the same accounts. Referral track is an option if you want to test the relationship first.

**Is there a partner fee?**  
No annual fee. Revenue share model only — you earn when we earn.

**How is commission calculated?**  
Percentage of customer's subscription value, paid monthly for the life of the account. You keep earning as long as they stay.

**What support do you provide for implementations?**  
Implementation playbooks, remote training support, and escalation paths. For complex projects, our services team can assist at partner margin.
