# Clinical EMR & SOAP notes

## Hero
**H1:** Notes that finish before the patient leaves the room.
**Subhead:** Specialty templates, voice assist, and pullforward — documentation designed by working clinicians, not compliance officers.

## Key capabilities
- **SOAP workspace:** Two-panel consult view — history, vitals, results on the left; today's note on the right. Nothing more than two clicks away.
- **42 specialty packs:** Structured templates per specialty — partograph for OBG, joint exam charts for ortho, TNM staging for onco — each with matched order sets.
- **Longitudinal timeline:** Every encounter, result, prescription, and document on one scrollable patient timeline with filters.
- **Order sets:** Specialty- and condition-level bundles (investigations + meds + advice) applied in one click, editable inline.
- **Voice & AI assist:** Dictation to structured note, coding suggestions (ICD-10), abnormal-history flagging. Doctor always reviews before sign.
- **Digital signatures & audit:** NMC-compliant sign-off, full version history, NABH-ready audit trail.
