## The problems we built around
- **Symptoms are the vital signs here:** Pain/ESAS scores must trend like blood pressure does elsewhere.
- **Care happens at home:** Home-visit teams need mobile, offline-tolerant documentation.
- **Opioid access demands paperwork discipline:** Morphine licensing and registers are the gateway to pain relief in India.

## How palliative care runs on AarogyaEHR
### Symptom assessment engine
ESAS/pain scores each contact, trended against interventions; caregiver-reported scores via WhatsApp between visits.
### Home-care module
Visit routes and schedules for field teams, offline mobile documentation, supply (catheter/wound) kit tracking.
### Opioid & NDPS documentation
Morphine prescription and stock registers per state NDPS rules, consumption reconciliation, renewal alerts.
### Goals of care & directives
Structured goals-of-care discussions, advance-directive capture, DNAR documentation visible on the patient banner.
### Geriatric assessment
Comprehensive geriatric assessment templates, polypharmacy review with deprescribing prompts, falls-risk and cognition screens.
