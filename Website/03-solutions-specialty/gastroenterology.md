## The problems we built around
- **Endoscopy reports lag the list:** High-volume lists produce report backlogs and lost images.
- **Hepatology is trend medicine:** LFTs, viral loads, and fibrosis scores scattered across visits hide the trajectory.
- **Scope reprocessing is an audit landmine:** Disinfection logs on paper fail NABH scrutiny.

## How gastroenterology runs on AarogyaEHR
### Endoscopy reporting suite
Procedure templates (UGIE, colonoscopy, ERCP, EUS) with anatomical image tagging, standardized terminology, instant printed/portal reports.
### Image & video capture
Endoscope capture into the episode; key frames annotated; comparisons across surveillance scopes.
### Hepatology panels
LFT/INR/viral-load trending, Child-Pugh and MELD auto-scores, variceal surveillance recalls, hepatitis-B antiviral monitoring.
### Scope reprocessing log
Per-scope disinfection cycles with operator sign-off — the register exists because the workflow creates it.
### GI surgery linkage
Endoscopy findings flow into surgical planning; operative notes for lap and open GI procedures.
