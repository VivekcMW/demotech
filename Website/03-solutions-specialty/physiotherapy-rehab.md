# Physiotherapy & rehabilitation (PMR)

## Hero
**H1:** Recovery is a program, not an appointment.

## The problems we built around
- **Therapy compliance is invisible upstream:** Surgeons can't see whether prescribed rehab actually happened.
- **Plans drift without protocols:** Post-TKR week-3 should look the same across therapists.
- **Sessions are a scheduling business:** Therapist utilization and package balances need management.

## How physiotherapy runs on AarogyaEHR
### Protocol library
Condition/surgery-specific rehab protocols (post-TKR, stroke, sports) with week-wise goals and exercise prescriptions.
### Session documentation
Quick per-session notes with pain scores, ROM measurements, modality logging; home-exercise prints with diagrams in the patient's language.
### Outcome measurement
Standardized scores (ROM grids, Berg, FIM) trended across the program; discharge-from-therapy criteria.
### Scheduling & packages
Therapist-wise calendars, session-package billing with balances, gym/equipment slot management.
### Referral loop
Referring surgeon/physician sees attendance and outcome trends on their own follow-up screen.
