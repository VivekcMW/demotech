# PRD 1.8 — Nursing Workflows
## Indian EHR System — Nursing Tasking, Charting, and Handover

**Document ID:** PRD-CORE-1.8  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** June 10, 2026

## 1. Overview

Defines nursing execution workflows for OPD/IPD/ICU/daycare including task lists, eMAR-linked administration events, vitals charting, care plans, and handovers.

### Scope
- Shift-based nursing task boards
- Vitals and observations charting
- Medication administration execution (from orders)
- Intake/output and nursing assessments
- SBAR handover workflows
- Fall risk, pressure injury, pain score tracking

## 2. Personas
- Staff nurse
- Nurse in-charge
- ICU nurse
- Ward supervisor
- Duty doctor (consumer of nursing data)

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Shift Worklist
- Auto-generated from active orders and care plans
- Prioritized tasks with due times and escalation labels
- Bulk acknowledge/start/complete actions

### 3.2 Vitals & Observations
- Configurable frequency by unit/patient acuity
- Range checks and abnormal highlighting
- Device-fed optional data ingestion

### 3.3 Medication Administration
- Administration task queue from medication orders
- Document administered/held/missed/refused with reason
- Time-window compliance tracking

### 3.4 Care Plans & Assessments
- Nursing assessment templates
- Risk scores: falls, pressure ulcer, pain
- Care interventions and reassessment intervals

### 3.5 Intake/Output
- Fluids input and output charting
- Shift and 24-hour totals
- Alert for critical imbalances as configured

### 3.6 Handover
- SBAR template handover by patient and ward
- Pending critical tasks section mandatory
- Handover sign-off by outgoing and incoming staff

## 4. Data Model
- `NURSING_TASK`
- `VITAL_ENTRY`
- `MED_ADMIN_EVENT`
- `NURSING_ASSESSMENT`
- `CARE_PLAN`
- `IO_ENTRY`
- `HANDOVER_NOTE`

## 5. Workflows
1. Shift start → review task list → execute + chart → close shift
2. Medication due → administer/exception record → escalation if overdue
3. Handover generation → SBAR review → dual sign-off

## 6. Integrations
- `1.4` Orders (task generation)
- `1.3` Documentation (flowsheets/notes)
- `1.7` ADT (patient location and transfer changes)
- `1.11` Notifications and audit

## 7. Reports
- Medication administration compliance
- Overdue nursing tasks
- Handover completion SLA
- Falls/pressure injury/pain assessment adherence

## 8. NFRs
- Task board refresh < 3 sec
- Vitals save < 300 ms
- Offline-safe charting for temporary network loss

## 9. Acceptance Criteria
1. Active orders produce nurse task items correctly
2. All med exceptions require reason capture
3. Handover cannot close without pending critical task review
4. Risk assessments follow configured frequencies
5. Nursing actions fully auditable
