## The problems we built around
- **Volume kills documentation:** At 5 minutes a patient, any EMR slower than paper gets abandoned by week two.
- **Chronic patients arrive as strangers:** Diabetics and hypertensives return monthly, but their history lives in carry-bags of old files.
- **Follow-ups leak revenue and outcomes:** No system tracks who didn't come back; the patient and the revenue both disappear.

## How general medicine runs on AarogyaEHR
### Two-minute consult notes
Complaint-driven templates with pullforward of the last visit — review, modify, sign. Vitals flow in from the nursing station.
### Chronic disease registry
Auto-enrolled diabetes/hypertension/thyroid registries with trend panels (HbA1c, BP, lipids) and due-for-review lists.
### One-click order sets
Fever panel, diabetic annual workup, pre-employment checkup — investigations, meds, and advice in one tap, editable inline.
### Follow-up engine
Every consult ends with a follow-up date; reminder cascades on WhatsApp/SMS in the patient's language; defaulter lists for counselors.
