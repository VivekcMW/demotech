## Who this is for

Pathology labs (standalone and chains), imaging centers, integrated diagnostic centers, and hospital labs that serve outside (B2B) work. Volume ranges from 100 to 10,000+ samples a day; the failure modes are the same — transcription errors, TAT slippage, and B2B billing disputes.

---

## The problems we solve at this scale

**Transcription kills accuracy and TAT.** Manual result entry is where wrong reports come from. {Product}'s LIS interfaces analysers two-way (ASTM/HL7): orders go to the machine, results return validated, deltas and criticals flag automatically, and the pathologist signs by exception.

**Home collection is now table stakes.** Phlebotomist app with route lists, patient OTP verification, barcode-at-doorstep, and temperature-logged handover — collection-to-accession is one chain of custody.

**B2B reconciliation fights.** Referring labs and hospitals dispute volumes and rates monthly. Rate cards per client, automatic monthly statements, and a client portal where they see their own ledger end the argument.

**NABL evidence.** IQC (Levey-Jennings, Westgard rules), EQAS records, calibration logs, and TAT audits are maintained as you work — assessment week stops being a crisis.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: We process for 40 collection centers. Can they book and print at their end?**
A: Yes — collection-center logins with your rate card, barcode printing at source, sample manifests for logistics, and automatic ledger entries per center.

**Q: Our senior pathologist wants to verify everything; juniors should auto-verify normals. Possible?**
A: Yes — auto-verification rules by test, by delta, and by flag, with everything abnormal routed to the verification queue. The rules are yours, and the audit trail records every release.

**Q: Do patients get raw PDFs on WhatsApp?**
A: They get a secure link with OTP — reports stay current (amended reports update at the link), and you control downloads. Direct PDF delivery is available where you prefer it.
