## The problems we built around
- **TB care is protocol + adherence:** NTEP/DOTS documentation and follow-up discipline decide outcomes and compliance.
- **Spirometry sits in printouts:** Serial lung function belongs on a curve, not in a drawer.
- **Asthma patients live between visits:** Without action plans and inhaler-technique follow-through, control fails at home.

## How pulmonology runs on AarogyaEHR
### TB & NTEP workflows
Notification-ready documentation, regimen tracking with adherence logs, contact-screening lists, Nikshay-aligned exports.
### Spirometry & PFT trending
Device-report capture with FEV1/FVC longitudinal curves, pre/post bronchodilator comparisons, GOLD/GINA staging support.
### Asthma/COPD programs
Action plans printed in the patient's language, inhaler-technique checklists, exacerbation episode tracking, vaccination prompts.
### Bronchoscopy suite
Procedure reports with image capture, BAL/sample handoff to LIS, sedation records inline.
