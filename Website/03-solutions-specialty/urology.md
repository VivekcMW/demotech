## The problems we built around
- **Stone recurrence is the rule:** Without imaging comparisons and metabolic workups on file, every episode starts from zero.
- **Endourology hardware adds up:** Stents in, stents forgotten — retained DJ stents are a real medico-legal risk.
- **Prostate follow-up spans years:** PSA trends and post-TURP outcomes need longitudinal discipline.

## How urology runs on AarogyaEHR
### Stone disease pathway
Imaging-compared episodes, stone size/location mapping, ESWL/URS/PCNL procedure notes, metabolic workup templates and prevention advice prints.
### DJ stent registry
Every stent placement logs a removal-due date; overdue stents alarm until resolved — retained-stent risk engineered out.
### Uroflow & urodynamics
Device report capture with trend views; IPSS questionnaires from the patient app pre-visit.
### Prostate care
PSA trending with velocity, biopsy documentation (with histopath loop), TURP operative notes, post-op voiding follow-ups.
### Uro-oncology & access surgery
Bladder tumor surveillance cystoscopy recalls; AV fistula creation notes linked to the Nephrology access timeline.
