# Laboratory (LIS)

## Hero
**H1:** From sample to signed report without a single register.
**Subhead:** Barcode-driven LIS with analyzer interfacing, critical-value escalation, and reports patients can actually read.

## Key capabilities
- **Order to collection:** Orders flow from EMR; phlebotomy worklists, barcode label printing, sample tracking with chain of custody.
- **Analyzer interfacing:** Bi-directional interfaces for common analyzers; results land with flags, no manual entry.
- **Flag system:** HH/H/N/L/LL flags with delta checks against the patient's previous values; critical results trigger escalation calls logged with timestamp + acknowledger (NABH requirement).
- **Validation & sign-off:** Two-level verification, pathologist e-sign, automatic re-run tracking.
- **Smart reports:** Cumulative trend view, reference ranges by age/sex, bilingual report headers, WhatsApp/portal delivery with consent.
