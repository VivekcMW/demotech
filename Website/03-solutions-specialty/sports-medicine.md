## How sports medicine runs on AarogyaEHR
### Injury episode management
Mechanism-coded injury records, imaging linkage, treatment timelines from field to clinic.
### Return-to-play engine
Sport-specific staged criteria with objective test gates (strength symmetry, hop tests); clearance documentation with sign-off.
### Performance & baseline testing
Pre-season screening batteries, baseline concussion testing (SCAT), comparisons at injury time.
### Team coverage
Squad rosters with availability boards, event-coverage notes, anti-doping-aware prescribing flags (WADA reference checks).
### Rehab integration
Physio protocols with load-progression logging shared between clinic and training staff.
