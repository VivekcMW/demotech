## The problems we built around
- **The pump record is its own discipline:** Perfusion data belongs in the episode, not a separate logbook.
- **Post-op cardiac ICU is minute-by-minute:** Drains, inotropes, and pacing settings need dense, fast charting.
- **Valves and grafts are lifetime commitments:** Prosthesis details and anticoagulation targets must outlive the admission.

## How CTVS runs on AarogyaEHR
### Cardiac surgical documentation
CABG/valve operative templates with conduit mapping, prosthesis barcode capture into the valve registry.
### Perfusion record
Bypass-time charting, cardioplegia logs, ACT trends — integrated into the operative episode.
### Cardiac ICU continuity
Post-op flowsheets with drain outputs, inotrope scales, pacing parameters; extubation and step-down criteria checklists.
### Anticoagulation clinic
INR-targeted follow-up for valve patients with dose-adjustment history and recall discipline.
### Vascular workflows
Aneurysm surveillance imaging recalls, bypass/EVAR documentation, diabetic-foot revascularization linkage.
