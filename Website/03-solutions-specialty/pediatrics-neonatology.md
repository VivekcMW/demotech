## The problems we built around
- **Dosing errors are the biggest pediatric risk:** Adult-style prescribing screens make mg/kg mistakes easy under OPD pressure.
- **Immunization tracking is fragmented:** IAP/NIS schedules, catch-up plans, and stock all live in different registers.
- **Growth faltering hides in single visits:** Without plotted longitudinal charts, the falling centile goes unnoticed.

## How pediatrics runs on AarogyaEHR
### Weight-based prescribing guards
Every drug calculates from current weight with per-kg and max-dose ceilings; out-of-range doses trigger hard stops.
### Growth & development tracking
WHO/IAP growth charts auto-plotted each visit, centile-crossing alerts, development milestone checklists by age.
### Immunization engine
IAP and NIS schedules with due/overdue lists, catch-up plan generation, vaccine batch capture, and parent reminders in their language.
### NICU flowsheets
Hourly neonatal monitoring, fluid calculations per kg per day, phototherapy and respiratory support logs, daily-weight trend on the dark-theme ICU view.
### Pediatric OPD speed
Complaint templates for fever/ARI/diarrhea with danger-sign checklists aligned to IMNCI thinking.
