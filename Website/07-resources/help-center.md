# Help Center

## Structure

**By role** (the primary navigation — because a nurse and a billing executive never need the same article): Front Desk · Doctors · Nursing · Pharmacy · Lab · Radiology · Billing & TPA · Administration · IT

**By module** (secondary): mirrors the 14 product modules

**Article standard:** task-titled ("Cancel a bill after finalisation"), under 400 words, numbered steps, current screenshots, one short video where the flow is visual, last-verified date on every article.

## Beyond articles

- **Release notes** — every update, in plain language, with screenshots of what changed
- **Training paths** — sequenced courses per role for onboarding new staff (hospitals churn staff; training must be self-serve and repeatable)
- **Status page** — live platform status, linked from the Help Center header
- **Support routes** — in-app chat, WhatsApp support line, phone for priority issues; response-time commitments by plan published here
