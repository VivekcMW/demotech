# IPD, wards & bed board

## Hero
**H1:** Every bed, every patient, one live board.
**Subhead:** Admission to discharge with the ward-level visibility your nursing superintendent has been asking for.

## Key capabilities
- **Live bed board:** Ward-wise grid with status colors — occupied stable/watch/critical, vacant clean, needs cleaning, blocked. Click any bed for the patient mini-card.
- **Admission & transfer:** Bed allotment with class-wise tariff mapping, intra-ward and ICU transfers with automatic tariff change, attendant pass printing.
- **Nursing workflows:** Shift-wise task lists, vitals entry on tablets, medication administration records (eMAR) with barcode verification, intake-output charts.
- **Doctor rounds:** Ward list by consultant, progress notes with pullforward, order entry at bedside.
- **Discharge engine:** Checklist-driven — pharmacy returns, final bill, summary sign-off — with bottleneck visibility. Discharge summaries auto-assembled from the stay record, printed bilingual.
