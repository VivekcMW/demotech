# ROI Calculator

## Concept

An interactive calculator that estimates what {Product} is worth to a specific facility — with every assumption visible and adjustable, because hidden math is how vendors lie.

## Inputs

Facility type · bed count · average occupancy · OPD volume/day · IPD billing/month · cashless share % · current TPA receivable days · pharmacy revenue/month · number of doctors

## Outputs (each with the formula shown)

1. **Revenue-leakage recovery** — unbilled services and charge-capture gap, modelled conservatively at the low end of observed ranges (with the range cited)
2. **TPA cycle compression** — working-capital value of cutting receivable days, at the user's own cost of funds
3. **Pharmacy recovery** — expiry, dead stock, and reconciliation gains
4. **Documentation time returned** — doctor and nursing hours/month, valued at user-set rates
5. **Cost of {Product}** — the actual subscription estimate for their inputs, side by side; the calculator shows our price next to the benefit, which most vendors won't

**Output format:** annual ₹ figures in lakh/crore formatting, a payback-period estimate, and a downloadable one-page PDF of their scenario.

## Honesty rules

Defaults are conservative; ranges shown, not point claims; an explicit footnote that results depend on adoption and process discipline, not software alone.
