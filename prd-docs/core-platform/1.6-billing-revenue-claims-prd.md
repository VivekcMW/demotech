# PRD 1.6 — Billing, Revenue & Claims
## Indian EHR System — Unified Financial Workflow

**Document ID:** PRD-CORE-1.6  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** June 10, 2026

## 1. Overview

Defines end-to-end billing for OPD/IPD/daycare/OT/diagnostics with support for cash, corporate, TPA, Ayushman Bharat, CGHS, ESI, and mixed-payer episodes.

### Scope
- Charge capture from appointments/orders/procedures/pharmacy/bed usage
- OPD and IPD billing
- Package billing
- Deposit/advance handling
- Discount approvals
- Claim pre-auth, submission, adjudication tracking
- Refunds and reversals
- GST-compliant invoicing

## 2. Personas
- Billing executive
- Insurance/TPA desk
- Finance manager
- Front desk
- Patient/caregiver

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Charge Capture
- Real-time charges from modules `1.2`, `1.4`, `1.5`, `1.7`
- Manual add-ons with approval trails
- Prevention of duplicate charge posting

### 3.2 Tariff & Payer Rules
- Tariff master by payer, facility, specialty
- Effective-dated pricing
- Package vs itemized conflict rules
- Co-pay and coverage cap enforcement

### 3.3 OPD Billing
- Consultation + diagnostics + pharmacy + procedures
- Immediate invoice and receipt generation
- Split payment modes (cash/card/UPI)

### 3.4 IPD Billing
- Interim bills and final discharge bill
- Bed, nursing, consumables, procedures, pharmacy, diagnostics aggregation
- Credit/debit note workflow

### 3.5 Package Billing
- Maternity, surgery, dialysis, chemo, health check packages
- Included/excluded item logic
- Breach handling (outside package items)

### 3.6 Insurance & Govt Claims
- Pre-auth request lifecycle
- Document checklist and deficiency tracker
- Claim submission with line-item mapping
- Status tracking: submitted/query/approved/partially approved/rejected/settled

### 3.7 Discounts & Approvals
- Role-based discount matrix
- Mandatory reason and approver trail

### 3.8 Refunds & Reversals
- Full/partial refunds
- Approval matrix by amount thresholds
- Reversal linkage to original receipt

## 4. Data Model
- `TARIFF_MASTER`
- `CHARGE_EVENT`
- `BILL_HEADER`
- `BILL_LINE_ITEM`
- `PAYMENT_TRANSACTION`
- `CLAIM_CASE`
- `PREAUTH_CASE`
- `DISCOUNT_APPROVAL`
- `REFUND_EVENT`

## 5. Workflows
1. Clinical event → charge event → bill assembly
2. IPD interim bill cycles → final bill at discharge
3. Pre-auth → claim submission → adjudication → settlement posting

## 6. Integrations
- Payment gateway
- Accounting/ERP export
- Ayushman/TPA endpoints (where available)
- Module-wise charge feeds

## 7. Reports
- Revenue by department/payer
- AR aging and claim aging
- Rejection reason analytics
- Package profitability
- Discount leakage report

## 8. NFRs
- Invoice generation < 3 sec
- Bill recompute < 5 sec for 1k line items
- 100% financial audit traceability

## 9. Acceptance Criteria
1. Charges auto-post from source modules
2. Final IPD bill matches event ledger
3. Claim lifecycle trackable end-to-end
4. Discount/refund actions always approval-controlled
5. GST invoice and receipt outputs valid
