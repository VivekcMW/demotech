# OT & anaesthesia management

## Hero
**H1:** An OT schedule that survives the 7 am emergency.
**Subhead:** Slot management, PAC, WHO checklist, and consumption capture — so every case is documented and every implant is billed.

## Key capabilities
- **OT scheduling:** Theatre-wise calendar, emergency slot insertion with cascade re-planning, surgeon/anaesthetist/equipment conflict checks.
- **Pre-anaesthesia checkup:** Structured PAC with ASA grading, airway assessment, fitness sign-off gating the schedule.
- **Safety checklist:** WHO surgical safety checklist (sign-in / time-out / sign-out) enforced digitally with role-wise confirmations — NABH auditors love this.
- **Intra-op record:** Anaesthesia chart, drug and event timeline, implant and consumable capture by barcode (flows straight to billing).
- **Post-op:** Recovery scoring, surgeon's operative notes from templates, histopath sample handoff to LIS.

Zero unbilled implants · checklist compliance reports · turnover-time analytics per theatre
