# Careers

## The pitch, honestly

The work is hard in interesting ways: clinical workflows are unforgiving, hospitals run 24×7, and a bug here isn't an inconvenience — it's a wrong dose not caught. If consequence motivates you, this is the most consequential B2B software in India.

## How we work

Hospital-first product development (every product person does ward time during onboarding), small teams with real ownership, written decision-making, and a strong design-system culture — clinical UI is engineered, not decorated. Remote-flexible with periodic on-site weeks at customer hospitals.

## Teams we hire into

1. **Engineering** — product engineers (web, mobile), platform/infra, integrations (HL7/FHIR/DICOM), data
2. **Design** — product designers who can hold a design system and a nurse's 12-hour shift in their head at once
3. **Clinical informatics** — doctors, nurses, and pharmacists who'd rather fix the system than fight it
4. **Implementation & success** — the people who make go-lives succeed; hospital-floor temperament required
5. **Sales & marketing** — consultative, demo-led, allergic to overpromising

## Hiring process

Application → conversation → practical exercise drawn from real work (paid where substantial) → team interviews → offer. We tell you where you stand at every step, and 'no' comes with reasons.
