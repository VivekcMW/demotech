## 1. GOAL

Deliver a production-ready **Pediatrics** specialty implementation that supports:
- single-doctor pediatric clinic,
- multi-doctor pediatric OPD,
- single-branch pediatric unit,
- multi-branch pediatric network.

The implementation must enforce pediatric-safe defaults (age/weight-aware care), caregiver-centric workflows, and robust vaccination + growth surveillance.

---

## 2. RELEASE OBJECTIVES

### R1 (MVP)
- Pediatric OPD workflow (new + follow-up)
- Structured pediatric consultation templates
- Growth charting (WHO/IAP references)
- Immunization scheduling and due reminders
- Weight-based dosing checks for prescriptions

### R2
- Pediatric emergency/triage flows and escalation
- Inpatient pediatric documentation (admission, daily rounds, discharge)
- Neonatal and infant high-risk follow-up pathways
- Antibiotic stewardship and adverse-event tracking

### R3
- Multi-branch vaccine inventory coordination
- Population-level child health analytics (coverage, growth risk, follow-up adherence)
- Cross-branch continuity and referral orchestration

## 3. EPICS & USER STORIES

## Epic PED-01: Pediatric Consultation Templates

### Story PED-01-01
**As a** pediatrician, **I want** a structured new-visit template **so that** assessment is complete and standardized.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Mandatory fields include age, weight, height/length, head circumference (age-dependent), vitals, feeding history (for infants), developmental concerns.
2. Note cannot be signed without primary diagnosis and clinical plan.
3. Age-band specific sections render automatically (neonate/infant/child/adolescent).

### Story PED-01-02
**As a** pediatrician, **I want** a follow-up template with trend context **so that** longitudinal care is faster and safer.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Previous growth percentiles and recent diagnoses appear inline.
2. Copy-forward content is visibly marked and must be reviewed before sign.
3. Unreviewed critical history sections block final sign.

---

## Epic PED-02: Growth Monitoring & Developmental Surveillance

### Story PED-02-01
**As a** clinician, **I want** automated growth plotting **so that** undernutrition/obesity risk is detected early.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Weight-for-age, height-for-age, BMI-for-age charts available per age/sex.
2. Z-score/percentile trend is viewable across visits.
3. Out-of-range trend generates configurable clinical alert.

### Story PED-02-02
**As a** clinician, **I want** developmental milestone tracking **so that** delays are identified and referred early.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Age-bucket milestone checklist is available in consult note.
2. Suspected delay triggers referral suggestion workflow.
3. Milestone status is reportable at patient and population level.

---

## Epic PED-03: Immunization Lifecycle

### Story PED-03-01
**As a** pediatric OPD team member, **I want** vaccine due forecasting **so that** children receive timely vaccinations.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Vaccine schedule supports national + configurable local schedule overlays.
2. Due/overdue status shown at registration, consult, and discharge points.
3. Next due date auto-calculated after vaccination entry.

### Story PED-03-02
**As an** administrator, **I want** immunization reminder workflows **so that** missed doses are reduced.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Reminder notifications (SMS/WhatsApp) trigger for due and overdue doses.
2. Notification logs capture sent/delivered/failed status.
3. Opt-out rules respect policy while preserving critical-care messaging exceptions.

---

## Epic PED-04: Pediatric Medication Safety

### Story PED-04-01
**As a** doctor, **I want** weight-based dose calculators **so that** prescribing errors are minimized.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Dose recommendations are generated using current recorded weight.
2. Hard warning appears if entered dose exceeds configured safe range.
3. Prescription cannot be signed when unresolved high-severity warning exists.

### Story PED-04-02
**As a** pharmacist, **I want** pediatric dispensing verification **so that** incorrect formulation/strength is prevented.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Formulation appropriateness check (e.g., syrup/tablet/injectable) runs before dispense.
2. Duplicate active medication warning displayed.
3. Override requires reason and is fully audited.

---

## Epic PED-05: Pediatric Emergency & Inpatient Workflows

### Story PED-05-01
**As an** emergency clinician, **I want** pediatric triage and red-flag escalation **so that** critical children are fast-tracked.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Triage category is mandatory for emergency encounters.
2. Red-flag findings trigger stat escalation notifications.
3. Escalation acknowledgment time is tracked and reportable.

### Story PED-05-02
**As an** inpatient pediatrician, **I want** admission-to-discharge documentation bundle **so that** IPD care is consistent.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Admission note includes diagnosis, severity, hydration/nutrition risk, and immediate plan.
2. Daily progress note enforces pediatric vitals and response-to-treatment documentation.
3. Discharge summary requires diagnosis, meds, danger signs, and follow-up date.

---

## Epic PED-06: Caregiver-Centric Communication

### Story PED-06-01
**As a** caregiver, **I want** clear visit summaries and instructions **so that** home care adherence improves.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Visit summary includes diagnosis explanation, dose instructions, warning signs, and follow-up plan.
2. Content available in configured language(s).
3. Delivery via print, portal, and approved messaging channels.

### Story PED-06-02
**As a** care team member, **I want** caregiver consent and identity linkage **so that** communication and access stay compliant.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Guardian relationship and authorization are captured for minors.
2. Access logs reflect caregiver interactions.
3. Consent updates are versioned and auditable.

---

## Epic PED-07: Multi-Branch Governance & Analytics

### Story PED-07-01
**As a** central quality lead, **I want** branch-level pediatric KPI benchmarking **so that** quality variation is managed.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. KPI board includes immunization coverage, follow-up adherence, emergency escalation SLA, and documentation completeness.
2. Branch drill-down and trend view available.
3. Export to PDF/CSV available with role-based restrictions.

### Story PED-07-02
**As a** network clinician, **I want** cross-branch child record continuity **so that** treatment remains consistent across locations.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Cross-branch access respects consent + RBAC scope.
2. Referral and transfer summaries preserve diagnosis/medication/allergy context.
3. Cross-branch access events are audited.

## 6. UAT CHECKLIST

1. Pediatric new visit can be completed with all mandatory fields.
2. Growth chart percentiles and trends reflect visit data accurately.
3. Vaccine due forecasting and reminders work as configured.
4. Weight-based dosing warnings trigger for unsafe doses.
5. Emergency red-flag escalation reaches assigned responders.
6. IPD pediatric documentation satisfies mandatory compliance fields.
7. Caregiver summaries are understandable and correctly localized.
8. Cross-branch access and referrals honor consent + RBAC.
9. KPI outputs reconcile with transactional records.

---

## 7. RISKS & MITIGATIONS

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Incomplete pediatric vitals capture | Clinical safety risk | Mandatory fields + role-based hard-stops |
| Vaccine schedule variability across facilities | Operational inconsistency | Central schedule baseline + controlled local overlays |
| Alert fatigue from dose/growth warnings | Reduced actionability | Severity tiers + governance tuning |
| Guardian identity mismatch | Privacy/compliance risk | Strong guardian linkage + consent verification workflow |

## 8. DEFINITION OF DONE

2. Pediatric safety validations (dose/growth/triage as applicable) pass.
3. Audit logging and access control checks are verified.
4. Reporting impacts are tested where applicable.
5. Clinical + nursing + operations UAT sign-off is completed.
