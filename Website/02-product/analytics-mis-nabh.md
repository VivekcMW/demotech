# Analytics, MIS & NABH reports

## Hero
**H1:** Monday-morning numbers without the Sunday-night Excel.
**Subhead:** Live dashboards for promoters, department KPIs for HODs, and NABH quality indicators generated from real workflow data.

## Key capabilities
- **Promoter dashboard:** Revenue by department/doctor/payer, occupancy, ALOS, OT utilization, OPD-to-IPD conversion — daily, on mobile.
- **Departmental KPIs:** TAT for lab/radiology, discharge time, ED door-to-doctor, pharmacy stockouts.
- **NABH quality indicators:** The standard indicator set computed from system events (not retrospective registers) — medication errors, falls, CAUTI/VAP documentation hooks, consent compliance.
- **Statutory & scheme reports:** Birth/death registers, notifiable disease reporting, PM-JAY utilization.
- **Custom report builder:** Drag-drop measures with export; scheduled email/WhatsApp delivery.
