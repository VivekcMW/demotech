# Daycare workflows

## Hero
**H1:** Short stays, tight turnarounds, full documentation.
**Subhead:** Dialysis, chemo, endoscopy, cataract — the highest-volume service lines in Indian hospitals get first-class workflows, not adapted IPD forms.

## Key capabilities
- **Slot & chair/machine scheduling:** Session calendars per machine/chair/theatre with recurring bookings (MWF dialysis patterns), standby lists.
- **Session documentation:** Specialty flowsheets — dialysis parameters, chemo administration with regimen day tracking, endoscopy findings with image capture, cataract pre/post-op checklists.
- **Package billing:** Session packages, scheme mapping (PM-JAY dialysis), auto-consumption of consumables per session.
- **Recurring-patient care:** Longitudinal session history, missed-session alerts, cumulative dose/exposure tracking.
- **Throughput analytics:** Chair utilization, turnover time, cancellation patterns.
