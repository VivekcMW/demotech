## The problems we built around
- **Registration before triage costs lives:** Demographics-first systems invert clinical priority at the door.
- **MLC paperwork is high-stakes and chaotic:** Injury records, police intimation, and chain of custody fail under pressure.
- **Nobody can see the department state:** Who's waiting on what — results, beds, decisions — lives in shouted updates.

## How emergency runs on AarogyaEHR
### Triage-first intake
Severity scoring before demographics; unknown-patient flows with later identity merge; triage-to-doctor time tracked automatically.
### Resuscitation mode
Single dark-theme screen: vitals, timed event log, weight-based emergency drugs, defibrillation/airway documentation for code review.
### MLC discipline
One-toggle MLC flagging adds injury body-maps, photographs, police intimation logging, and locked audit trails.
### ED tracking board
Bay-wise live board with time-in-department, pending investigations, disposition status; escalation colors per wait thresholds.
### Disposition workflows
Admit with bed request, refer with structured transfer notes, discharge with safety-netting advice in the patient's language.
