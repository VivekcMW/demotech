# Billing, GST, TPA & insurance

## Hero
**H1:** Every rupee of care, captured and collected.

## Key capabilities
- **OPD billing:** Service packages, doctor-wise tariffs, multiple payment modes incl. UPI, ₹ lakh/crore formatting with GST breakup.
- **IPD billing:** Class-wise tariff engine, interim bills, automatic service capture from orders (no missed charges), deposit and refund management with approvals.

- **Leakage control:** Unbilled-services report, order-to-bill audit, credit-note approval chains, day-end cash reconciliation.
