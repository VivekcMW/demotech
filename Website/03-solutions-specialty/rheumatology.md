## The problems we built around
- **Disease activity is a number:** DAS28/BASDAI trends drive therapy; computing them by hand each visit doesn't happen.
- **DMARDs and biologics need vigilance:** Labs, screenings (TB before biologics), and toxicity checks must be systematic.
- **Autoimmune disease is multi-system:** Joints today, kidneys tomorrow — the record must connect specialties.

## How rheumatology runs on AarogyaEHR
### Activity-score engine
Joint-count homunculus input, auto-computed DAS28/CDAI/BASDAI, treat-to-target trend lines against therapy changes.
### Drug-safety monitoring
DMARD lab schedules (CBC/LFT) with due alerts; pre-biologic screening checklists (CBNAAT/CXR/HBV); infusion records via Daycare.
### Multi-system documentation
Lupus/vasculitis organ-wise assessment grids; shared records with Nephrology for renal involvement.
### Patient-reported outcomes
HAQ and pain scores from the app pre-visit; flare reporting between visits.
### Steroid stewardship
Cumulative steroid tracking with taper plans and bone-protection prompts.
