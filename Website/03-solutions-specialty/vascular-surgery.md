## The problems we built around
- **Diabetic foot is a race against amputation:** Wound staging, revascularization timing, and multi-team coordination decide limbs.
- **Aneurysms grow silently between scans:** Surveillance intervals missed are ruptures risked.
- **Access surgery serves another department's patients:** Fistula work must sync with nephrology's schedules and records.

## How vascular surgery runs on AarogyaEHR
### Diabetic foot program
Wound staging with photo timelines, ABI/duplex result tracking, revascularization decision documentation, amputation-prevention metrics.
### Aneurysm surveillance
Diameter-trend registries with imaging-interval recalls; threshold-crossing alerts to the surgical worklist.
### Open & endo documentation
Bypass operative notes with conduit mapping; EVAR records with device details into the implant registry; contrast/radiation logging.
### Dialysis access service
Fistula/graft creation and intervention notes synced to the Nephrology access timeline; maturation tracking.
### Venous & varicose care
Duplex-mapped varicose documentation, ablation procedure records, compression-therapy follow-ups.
