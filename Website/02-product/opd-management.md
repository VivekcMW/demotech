# OPD management & queue

## Hero
**H1:** Built for the 9 am OPD rush.
**Subhead:** Token to consult to pharmacy in one flow — for departments that see 60–100 patients per doctor per day.

## Key capabilities
- **Smart queue & tokens:** Walk-in + appointment mixed queues, department-wise token displays, priority tagging for seniors and follow-ups, average-wait visibility at the front desk.
- **Fast registration:** New patient in under 60 seconds — Aadhaar-assisted demographics, ABHA creation at the desk, duplicate detection by phone + name + age, transliteration search (Ram = राम).
- **Consult workspace:** Patient context on the left, SOAP note on the right. Specialty templates, quick-text in 9 languages, previous-visit pullforward.
- **Follow-up engine:** Auto follow-up scheduling, WhatsApp/SMS reminders, missed-visit lists for counselors.
- **Doctor-wise analytics:** Patients per hour, average consult time, conversion to investigations and admissions.

40-second average OPD bill · queue status visible on patient's phone · works on shared front-desk PCs
