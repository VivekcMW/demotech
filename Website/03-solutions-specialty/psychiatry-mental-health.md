## The problems we built around
- **Stigma demands stricter privacy:** Psychiatric records need tighter access walls than general notes.
- **Progress is measured in scales:** PHQ-9, HAM-D, YMRS trends are the vital signs of this specialty.
- **MHCA-2017 compliance is specific:** Admission categories, consent capacity, and review documentation have statutory shapes.

## How psychiatry runs on AarogyaEHR
### Restricted-access records
Psychiatry notes sit behind specialty-level access controls with break-glass audit; receptionists see appointments, not diagnoses.
### Rating-scale engine
PHQ-9/GAD-7/HAM-D/YMRS administered in-app or in-clinic, scored and trended against medication changes.
### MHCA documentation
Independent/supported admission paperwork, capacity assessments, mandatory review schedules with due alerts.
### Tele-psychiatry
Scheduled video follow-ups with e-prescription rules for psychotropics handled per telemedicine guidelines.
### Therapy & liaison notes
Psychologist session records, structured liaison-psychiatry consults for IPD referrals, de-addiction program tracking.
