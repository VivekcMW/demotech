# Terms of Service

*Structured draft — section scope and the commitments we make publicly. Counsel to finalise.*

1. **Agreement & parties** — who contracts with whom; order forms and plan schedules incorporate into these terms
2. **The service** — subscription to {Product}; what's included per plan; implementation services as separately scoped work
3. **Customer data & ownership** — the hospital owns its data, full stop; our **exit-rights commitment**: complete structured export in standard formats, free, at any time including on termination
4. **Clinical responsibility boundary** — {Product} is a documentation, workflow, and decision-support tool; diagnosis and treatment decisions remain with licensed clinicians; AI outputs are drafts requiring human sign-off
5. **Acceptable use** — no unauthorised access attempts, no resale without partnership agreement, credential discipline, lawful use only
6. **Service levels** — uptime SLA by plan, service credits, scheduled-maintenance windows and notice, support response commitments (published in the Help Center)
7. **Fees & payment** — subscription billing, taxes, late-payment terms, the no-surprise rule: compliance features are never separately charged
8. **Term, suspension & termination** — renewal mechanics, cure periods, what suspension does and doesn't affect (read access to your data survives payment disputes; patient safety is not a collections lever)
9. **Confidentiality** — mutual obligations; patient data additionally governed by the data-processing terms
10. **Warranties & disclaimers** — what we promise about the service; what depends on customer infrastructure and discipline
11. **Liability** — caps and carve-outs as negotiated per agreement class
12. **Disputes & governing law** — Indian law; jurisdiction; escalation-before-litigation clause
