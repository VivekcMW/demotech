## The problems we built around
- **Statutory medicals follow the law's format:** Factory Act and industry-specific examinations have prescribed shapes and registries.
- **Hazard exposure drives the protocol:** Audiometry for noise, spirometry for dust — surveillance must map to exposure.
- **Corporates want population dashboards:** Fitness distributions and trend reports, never individual disclosures.

## How occupational health runs on AarogyaEHR
### Examination engines
Pre-employment/periodic/exit medical batteries configured per industry and role; fitness certification with restriction coding.
### Hazard-based surveillance
Exposure-group registries (noise/dust/chemical) with mandated test schedules — audiometry, spirometry, biological monitoring — and recall discipline.
### Statutory registers
Factory Act Form registers, occupational-disease notification drafts, injury (accident) records with lost-time tracking.
### Corporate dashboards
Anonymized cohort analytics for employers (DPDP-clean), absenteeism-health correlations, program ROI reporting.
### On-site OHC operations
Factory medical-room workflows, first-aid logs, emergency-referral documentation to the parent hospital.
