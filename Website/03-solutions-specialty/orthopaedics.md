## The problems we built around
- **Implants are a traceability and billing nightmare:** Which plate, which batch, which patient — answered from memory is a risk; unbilled is a loss.
- **Trauma documentation happens at 2 am:** X-ray, consent, OT note, and MLC paperwork pile up exactly when staffing is thinnest.
- **Rehab is where results are made or lost:** Surgery succeeds, then follow-up physio compliance is invisible to the surgeon.

## How orthopaedics runs on AarogyaEHR
### Fracture & trauma clinic
Injury documentation with body-map annotation, X-ray side-by-side comparison from PACS, cast/POP tracking with removal dates, MLC support.
### Implant traceability
Barcode capture of every implant with batch/lot, auto-billing, and a searchable registry for recalls and revision planning.
### Arthroplasty workflow
Pre-op templating notes, standardized THR/TKR operative notes, day-wise post-op pathway checklists, PROM score capture (KOOS/HHS) at follow-ups.
### Physio linkage
Post-op protocols auto-assign physiotherapy plans; session compliance is visible on the surgeon's follow-up screen.
### Spine & arthroscopy packs
Procedure-specific templates with neuro-status checklists and graft documentation.

## Frequently asked questions
**Does it support implant recall lookups?**
Search any batch/lot and retrieve every patient who received it, with contact workflows.

**Can outcome scores be collected from patients?**
PROMs go to the patient app/WhatsApp pre-visit and land scored in the follow-up note.

**How are MLC trauma cases handled?**
MLC flagging, injury documentation, and police-intimation logs come from the Emergency module and stay attached to the episode.
