## What front-desk teams ask us

**Q: Patients give different mobile numbers each visit. How do you stop duplicates?**
A: Fuzzy matching across name variants, age, address fragments, and number history, with a merge tool that preserves both histories when a duplicate slips through.

**Q: Can we take advance booking with payment online?**
A: Yes — patients book and pay through the patient app or a web link; the slot blocks instantly and the refund policy you set is enforced automatically.

**Q: Our front desk doubles as the phone exchange. Does the system help?**
A: Incoming-call screen-pop (with supported PBX integrations) shows the patient's record as the phone rings — bookings and queries get answered without asking the patient to repeat everything.
