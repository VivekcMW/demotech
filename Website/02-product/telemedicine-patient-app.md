# Telemedicine & patient app

## Hero
**H1:** Your hospital, in the patient's pocket.
**Subhead:** Video consults, reports, payments, and reminders — in the patient's own language.

## Key capabilities
- **Teleconsultation:** Scheduled and on-demand video visits per Telemedicine Practice Guidelines, e-prescription delivery, payment before consult.
- **Patient app & portal:** Appointments, queue position, lab reports with trend views, bills and payments, family profiles under one login.
- **ABHA-linked PHR:** Patients link ABHA and receive records via consent-based ABDM flows.
- **WhatsApp journeys:** Confirmations, queue alerts, report-ready notifications, payment links, follow-up nudges — in 9 languages.
- **Feedback & NPS:** Post-visit feedback capture, service-recovery flags routed to administrators.
