## Who this is for

Solo practitioners, group practices, and polyclinics with visiting consultants — typically 1 to 15 doctors, OPD-only or OPD plus minor procedures. If your current system is a paper register, a WhatsApp group, and a billing app that doesn't talk to either, this page is for you.

## The problems we solve at this scale

**The 4-minute consultation problem.** Indian OPD consultations are short. Any EMR that takes longer than paper loses. {Product}'s clinic EMR is built around specialty quick-templates, favourite prescriptions, and keyboard-first entry — most doctors complete a structured note and e-prescription in under 90 seconds.

**No-shows and lost follow-ups.** Patients who don't return are lost revenue and lost continuity. Automated WhatsApp and SMS reminders in the patient's language bring follow-ups back without front-desk effort.

**The visiting consultant tangle.** Polyclinics juggle consultant schedules, revenue shares, and separate prescription pads. {Product} handles per-doctor calendars, per-doctor billing heads, and automatic revenue-share reports.

**Records that walk out the door.** When notes live in paper files or a doctor's personal laptop, the clinic owns nothing. With {Product}, every visit is a structured, ABHA-linkable record the clinic retains — which also makes the practice itself more valuable.

You don't need an IT person. {Product} for clinics is cloud-hosted, works in any browser on any laptop or tablet you already own, and the front desk learns it in an afternoon. ## Frequently asked questions

**Q: We still write some prescriptions on paper. Can we go hybrid?**
A: Yes. Many clinics start with billing + appointments digital and move consultations to digital one doctor at a time. Scanned paper prescriptions can be attached to the visit so the record stays complete.

**Q: What happens to my data if I stop using {Product}?**
A: It's yours. Full structured export (patient master, visits, prescriptions, bills) in standard formats, free, whenever you ask.

**Q: Is ABHA mandatory for my patients?**
A: No. ABHA linking is optional per patient. {Product} works fully without it — but when a patient has an ABHA, registration becomes a 10-second scan.
