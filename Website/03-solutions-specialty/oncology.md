## The problems we built around
- **Chemo math is unforgiving:** BSA-based dosing, cumulative ceilings, and renal adjustments cannot live in calculators and memory.
- **Staging hides in free text:** Without structured TNM, registries, audits, and trials are reconstruction projects.
- **Multimodality care fragments:** Surgery, chemo, and radiation each keep their own story about the same patient.

## How oncology runs on AarogyaEHR
### Regimen library & dose engine
Protocol library (FOLFOX, AC-T, R-CHOP and beyond) with BSA/auc dose calculation, cumulative anthracycline tracking, renal/hepatic adjustment prompts, double-verification before release.
### Chemo daycare
Chair scheduling by regimen duration, pharmacy preparation worklists, administration records with vitals checkpoints, extravasation protocols one tap away.
### Structured staging
TNM (site-specific) captured as data with stage auto-derivation; restaging events on the timeline; registry exports.
### Tumor board
Case lists with imaging/path summaries, structured MDT decisions that become the active plan.
### Radiation oncology linkage
Simulation and fraction tracking, side-by-side with systemic therapy on one treatment calendar.
### Survivorship & palliative
Follow-up surveillance schedules per protocol; pain and symptom scales linking to Palliative care.
