## The problems we built around
- **Diabetes is managed on trends, charted as snapshots:** A single HbA1c means little; the five-year curve is the diagnosis.
- **Complication screening slips through:** Annual eye, foot, and kidney checks depend on the patient remembering.
- **Titration history is folklore:** Which insulin dose, changed when, why — reconstructed from memory at every visit.

## How diabetology runs on AarogyaEHR
### Trend-first review screen
HbA1c, FBS/PPBS, lipids, eGFR, and weight plotted on one panel with medication-change markers overlaid on the curves.
### Structured titration
Insulin and OHA changes logged as structured events with reasons; the regimen history reads like a story, not archaeology.
### Complication screening engine
Auto-scheduled annual fundus, foot exam (with monofilament checklist), urine ACR, and neuropathy screens; overdue lists drive recall calls.
### Diabetes education & programs
Diet and education material in the patient's language via the app/WhatsApp; program enrollment (90-day packages) with adherence tracking.
### Thyroid & endocrine packs
TSH titration tracking, growth/puberty workups, PCOS and osteoporosis templates with DEXA trending.
