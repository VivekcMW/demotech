## FAQ
**Is implementation charged separately?** One-time onboarding fee based on facility size; includes data migration, configuration, and on-site training.
**Do you charge for ABDM compliance?** No. Compliance is a baseline, not an upsell.
**Can we start with OPD and add IPD later?** Yes — plans upgrade in place, data carries over.
**On-premise option?** Available on Enterprise for hospitals with data-residency policies; we recommend our India-region cloud for most.
**Contract terms?** Annual billing default; multi-year discounts available. No per-patient or per-transaction charges.
