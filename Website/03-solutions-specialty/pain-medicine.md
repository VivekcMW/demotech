## The problems we built around
- **Pain is subjective; management can't be:** Validated scores must anchor every visit and intervention.
- **Interventions need imaging-grade records:** Fluoro/USG-guided blocks deserve procedure-suite documentation.
- **Opioid stewardship cuts both ways:** Under-treatment and misuse both stem from poor records.

## How pain medicine runs on AarogyaEHR
### Structured assessment
Pain scores (NRS/BPI), neuropathic screens (DN4), functional-impact scales trended across the program.
### Interventional suite
Block/RFA/epidural procedure templates with image capture, contrast and radiation logs, post-procedure response tracking at defined intervals.
### Medication stewardship
Opioid agreements, morphine-equivalent tracking, NDPS register linkage, taper plans with relapse flags.
### Multimodal programs
Physio, psychology (CBT), and intervention strands on one care plan with cross-visibility.
### Cancer-pain pathway
WHO-ladder progression documentation with Palliative-care continuity.

**Can referrers see outcomes?**
Referring surgeons/oncologists view response summaries on shared patients.
