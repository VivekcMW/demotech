## The problems we built around
- **Door-to-needle is won or lost in minutes:** Stroke thrombolysis decisions need NIHSS, imaging, and contraindications on one screen.
- **Epilepsy care is a diary problem:** Seizure frequency, drug levels, and titrations sprawl across years.
- **Neuro observations are high-frequency:** GCS/pupil charting every 15 minutes overwhelms paper.

## How neurosciences runs on AarogyaEHR
### Stroke code mode
Timestamped pathway from arrival: NIHSS scoring, CT result capture, thrombolysis checklist with contraindication gates, door-to-needle analytics.
### Epilepsy management
Seizure diaries (patient-app fed), AED titration history, drug-level trending, driving-advice documentation.
### Neuro observation charting
GCS, pupils, and limb-power flowsheets with deterioration alerts; works on ward tablets, dark theme for neuro-ICU nights.

Craniotomy/spine operative templates, implant capture, post-op neuro-obs protocols auto-assigned.

UPDRS/EDSS structured scores trending across visits; infusion-therapy scheduling via Daycare.
