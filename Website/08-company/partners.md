# Partners

{Product} reaches India's hospitals through people they already trust. The programme has four tracks:

## 1. Implementation partners

Healthcare IT firms and consultants who deploy {Product}: certified training, implementation playbooks, sandbox environments, and per-project economics. You own the relationship and the rollout; we back you with product and escalation support.

## 2. Regional resellers

Coverage-focused partners for states and city clusters, particularly Tier-2/3 markets where local presence wins deals: protected accounts, recurring revenue share on subscriptions (not one-time commissions), joint demos, and marketing support in regional languages.

## 3. Device & technology partners

Analyser, modality, and device manufacturers: certified interfaces in our integration library, joint go-to-market where the fit is real, and engineering collaboration on new instrument support.

## 4. Advisory & compliance partners

NABH consultants, ABDM enablers, and hospital-management advisors: referral economics, co-branded guides and webinars, and early access to compliance features your clients will ask about.

---

## What we ask of partners

Represent the product honestly (our no-overpromise rule extends to you), maintain certification, and protect customer data to the same standard we do. We drop partners who burn customer trust — that's in the agreement.
