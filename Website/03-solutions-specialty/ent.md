## The problems we built around
- **ENT OPDs run fast and procedural:** Wax removal to scopies happen mid-consult; documentation must keep pace.
- **Audiology data lives in another room:** Audiograms and consults rarely meet in one record.
- **Pediatric ENT is half the queue:** Tonsils, adenoids, and otitis need pediatric-aware templates and dosing.

## How ENT runs on AarogyaEHR
### Procedure-in-consult flow
In-chair procedures documented in seconds with auto-billing — no separate slip cycle.
### Endoscopy & imaging
Nasal/laryngeal scope image capture into the note; pre/post comparisons for surgical counseling.
### Audiology integration
Audiogram capture with serial comparison; hearing-aid trial and dispensing records; newborn hearing-screening recalls.
### Daycare ENT surgery
Tonsillectomy/FESS/myringoplasty pathways with same-day discharge criteria and post-op instruction prints in the family's language.

Structured vertigo workups, allergy-testing records with immunotherapy schedules.
