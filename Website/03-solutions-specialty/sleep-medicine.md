## The problems we built around
- **Sleep medicine begins with questionnaires:** ESS/STOP-BANG screening must flow into triage, not paper piles.
- **The lab is a scheduling and reporting unit:** Bed-nights, technician scoring, and physician sign-off form a pipeline.
- **CPAP success is a compliance program:** Devices without follow-up become bedside furniture.

## How sleep medicine runs on AarogyaEHR
### Screening & triage
App-administered ESS/STOP-BANG with risk-tiered routing to home study vs in-lab PSG.
### Sleep lab pipeline
Bed-night scheduling, study-night documentation, scoring worklists, physician interpretation templates with report delivery.
### Titration & prescription
CPAP/BiPAP titration records, pressure prescriptions, mask-fit documentation, device dispensing with inventory.
### Compliance program
Device-data uploads or self-reports trended; low-compliance outreach worklists; annual review recalls.
### Comorbidity loops
Referral integration with Pulmonology, ENT (airway assessment), Cardiology (resistant hypertension), Bariatric pathways.
