# Pharmacy & inventory

## Hero
**H1:** The pharmacy module your purchase head will audit and approve.
**Subhead:** Batch, expiry, schedule-drug registers, and ward indents — with zero tolerance for stock leakage.

## Key capabilities
- **Dispensing:** Prescription queue from OPD/IPD, barcode-verified dispensing, substitution rules (generic policy), counseling notes.
- **Inventory control:** Batch + expiry tracking with FEFO picking, reorder levels, near-expiry alerts, multi-store transfers (main store → ward sub-stores).

- **Procurement:** Indents, purchase orders, GRN with rate verification, supplier returns, GST input tracking.
- **Margin intelligence:** Item-wise margin, fast/slow movers, consumption by department — the leakage report promoters ask for.
