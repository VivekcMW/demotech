## The problems we built around
- **ART runs on cycles, not visits:** Stimulation days, scans, triggers, and procedures form a calendar no generic EMR understands.
- **Embryology is a chain of custody:** Gametes and embryos demand witness-verified, audit-proof records.
- **ART Act 2021 changed the rules:** Registration, consents, and registry reporting are now statutory.

## How IVF runs on AarogyaEHR
### Couple-linked records
Partner records linked with combined workup views, male-factor (andrology) integration, donor anonymization handling per law.
### Cycle management
Protocol calendars (antagonist/agonist) with day-wise medication, scan-folliculometry grids, trigger timing, OPU and ET scheduling.
### Embryology module
Oocyte/embryo tracking with grading, witness double-sign at every transfer point, cryostorage maps with location audit, thaw records.

Statutory consent set (couple/donor/surrogacy as applicable), National Registry reporting exports, age and eligibility guards.
