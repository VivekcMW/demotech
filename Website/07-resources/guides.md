# Guides

Substantial documents (15–40 pages) that are useful independent of {Product}. Flagship guides are ungated; only checklists and calculators ask for an email.

## The library

1. **The ABDM Implementation Guide for Private Hospitals** — ABHA, scan-and-share, HIP/HIU, consent architecture, incentives; the guide we wish had existed
2. **NABH Digital Evidence Handbook** — every entry-level and full-accreditation standard mapped to the digital records that satisfy it
3. **The DPDP Act Operations Manual for Healthcare** — consent, retention, breach readiness, staff training, in checklist form
4. **The HIMS Buyer's Guide** — 40 vendor questions, a scoring template, contract clauses to demand (including exit rights), and red flags; deliberately vendor-neutral
5. **The Revenue Integrity Workbook** — finding and closing leakage: unbilled services audit, discount governance, pharmacy reconciliation
6. **The TPA Desk Playbook** — pre-auth to settlement SOPs, payer-wise documentation checklists, deduction analytics
7. **The Daycare Economics Guide** — dialysis, chemo, endoscopy unit-economics models with scheme rates
8. **The Go-Live Survival Guide** — change management for hospital digitisation: sequencing, champions, the first 90 days

Each guide page: summary, table of contents, author credentials, download (PDF), and a related-webinar link.
