# Privacy Policy

*Plain-language summary first, full policy below — both are binding.*

## The summary

We process two kinds of data: **website/marketing data** about visitors and leads (we are the data fiduciary), and **patient data** processed inside the product on behalf of hospitals (the hospital is the fiduciary; we are the processor). We don't sell personal data. Patient data lives in India, encrypted. You can ask what we hold about you, correct it, or have it erased, subject to medical-record retention law.

## Sections (full policy)

1. **Who we are & scope** — entity details; what this policy covers (website, marketing, product telemetry) and what is governed by hospital agreements (patient records)
2. **Data we collect as fiduciary** — contact details from demo forms, usage analytics, support communications; lawful purpose for each
3. **Data we process as processor** — patient and clinical data under hospital instruction; our obligations: security, confidentiality, breach notification, no secondary use
4. **DPDP Act rights** — access, correction, erasure, grievance; how to exercise them; our grievance officer's contact; response timelines
5. **Consent & communications** — what we ask consent for, how to withdraw, transactional vs promotional messaging
6. **Security measures** — summary of the controls detailed on [Security & Data Privacy](/platform/security-data-privacy)
7. **Data residency & transfers** — Indian-region storage; conditions under which any cross-border processing would occur (and current position: none for patient data)
8. **Retention** — marketing-data timelines; patient-data retention follows medical-record law and hospital policy
9. **Third parties** — sub-processor categories (cloud hosting, communications gateways), with the current list available on request and contractual flow-down of obligations
10. **Children's data** — paediatric records are processed under hospital instruction with guardian consent captured by the hospital
11. **Changes & contact** — versioning, notice of material changes, grievance officer details
