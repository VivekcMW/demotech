## The problems we built around
- **Consent gaps surface in audits and courts:** Procedure-specific, language-appropriate consent is inconsistently documented.
- **Operative notes vary by surgeon and by mood:** Free-text notes miss details that billing, coding, and medico-legal review need.
- **Post-op events go untracked:** SSI and readmission data exist only if someone maintains a register.

## How general surgery runs on AarogyaEHR
### Pre-op workup & fitness
Checklist-driven workup with PAC gating, comorbidity flags, and antibiotic-prophylaxis prompts per policy.
### Consent management
Procedure-specific consent templates printed bilingual, signed digitally or scanned, version-locked to the episode.
### Structured operative notes
Procedure templates (hernia, appendix, cholecystectomy, thyroid, breast) with findings, technique, drains, and histopath handoff to LIS.
### Post-op pathway
Day-wise recovery checklists, wound documentation with photos, SSI surveillance prompts at follow-up, complication coding for NABH indicators.
### Daycare surgery flow
Short-stay admission, recovery scoring, same-day discharge criteria checklists.
