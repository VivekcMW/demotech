# e-Prescription & CDSS

## Hero
**H1:** Prescriptions your pharmacist never has to call about.
**Subhead:** Structured dosing, generic-first display, and a clinical decision support system that warns only when it matters.

## Key capabilities
- **Structured prescribing:** Drug search across generic + brand, strength/form/route/frequency/duration as structured fields — no free-text doses where structure exists.
- **CDSS that respects attention:** Four severity levels. Hard stops only for genuine danger (severe interaction, documented allergy); advisory notes stay quiet. Red keeps its meaning.
- **Allergy-first:** The allergy banner follows the patient everywhere and is checked on every drug add.
- **Bilingual prints:** NMC-compliant layout — generic emphasized, doctor registration number, instructions printed in English + the patient's language, dosage in ASCII numerals always.
- **Formulary & favorites:** Hospital formulary control, doctor-level favorite regimens, antibiotic policy flags for stewardship programs.

## Proof points
Interaction + allergy + duplicate-therapy checks on every add · prescription QR for pharmacy pickup · zero Devanagari numerals on doses, by design
