# Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. **The pilot promise** — new clinic and hospital customers start with a paid pilot period (length per plan); cancel within it and the subscription fee is refunded; scoped implementation work actually delivered is charged at the quoted rate
2. **Ongoing subscriptions** — cancel any time with notice (period per plan); service runs to the end of the paid period; no refunds for partial unused months, no cancellation penalties
3. **Annual prepaid plans** — pro-rata refund terms stated per plan schedule
4. **Implementation fees** — quoted fixed-price; milestone-based; refundable for milestones not yet delivered
5. **Your data on exit** — full structured export free of charge (see exit-rights commitment in Terms); data retained for a defined wind-down window for your retrieval, then securely deleted, subject to legal holds
6. **How to cancel** — written notice channels; confirmation timelines; no retention-call gauntlet
7. **Disputes** — billing-dispute process and timelines
