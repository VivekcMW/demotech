## The problems we built around
- **Selection is multidisciplinary:** Endocrine, psych, dietetic, and anaesthesia clearances must gate the OT date.
- **Outcomes are measured in years:** %EWL curves and comorbidity-resolution tracking need longitudinal discipline.
- **Nutrition deficiencies are the silent complication:** Lifelong supplement monitoring fails without recalls.

## How bariatric surgery runs on AarogyaEHR
### MDT workup gating
Checklist-driven clearances (endocrine/psych/diet/PAC) that must complete before scheduling unlocks; insurance pre-auth documentation pack assembly.
### Surgical documentation
Sleeve/bypass operative templates, stapler-load consumable capture, ERAS pathway checklists post-op.
### Weight & comorbidity tracking
%EWL/%TWL auto-curves, diabetes/hypertension medication-reduction timelines — the outcome story in graphs.
### Nutrition surveillance
Supplement protocols with annual micronutrient lab recalls (B12/iron/D), deficiency alerts.
### Program engagement
Support-group scheduling, diet-stage content via the app in the patient's language, plateau-intervention flags.
