# Blog

## Editorial position

Written for hospital owners, CMOs, administrators, and doctors in India. Every post must pass one test: would a busy hospital administrator forward this to a colleague? Practical over promotional; specific over visionary; Indian context always. Product mentions earn their place or don't appear.

## Content pillars

1. **Money** — revenue leakage, TPA receivables, pharmacy margins, scheme economics
2. **Compliance** — ABDM, NABH, DPDP, statutory registers, explained operationally
3. **Clinical operations** — OPD throughput, ICU documentation, medication safety
4. **Digitisation honestly** — what fails in HIMS projects and why; selection guides naming real trade-offs
5. **Specialty deep-dives** — one specialty's workflows per post (feeds the specialty SEO cluster)
