## The problems we built around
- **ANC visits scattered across registers:** High-risk flags get lost between the OPD card, the scan report, and the mother's file.
- **Labour-room documentation under pressure:** Partographs on paper are incomplete exactly when they matter most — medico-legally and clinically.
- **PC-PNDT compliance is manual and risky:** Form-F errors carry license-level consequences, yet most units still run on photocopies.

## Frequently asked questions
**Does the newborn get a linked record automatically?**
Yes — baby records are created at delivery, linked to the mother, with birth weight, APGAR, and immunization due-lists.

**Is Form-F legally compliant?**
The capture follows the statutory format with register exports; your radiologist/obstetrician reviews and signs digitally.

**Can ASHA/ANM referral data be captured?**
Referral-in source fields and high-risk referral documentation are part of the ANC record.
