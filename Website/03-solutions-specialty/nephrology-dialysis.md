## The problems we built around
- **Recurring care, episodic records:** MWF patients for five years generate hundreds of sessions nobody can trend on paper.
- **Machine scheduling is a whiteboard:** Slots, reuse cycles, and seropositive isolation managed by marker pen and memory.
- **Scheme billing is high-volume, low-margin:** PM-JAY dialysis runs on volume; manual package claims bleed it dry.

## How nephrology runs on AarogyaEHR
### HD session flowsheet
Pre/intra/post-dialysis parameters, heparin and UF tracking, complication logging — each session two minutes of structured entry on a chairside tablet.
### Machine & slot scheduling
Recurring MWF/TTS patterns per machine, seropositive machine segregation, reuse-cycle counters, standby lists for cancellations.
### CKD longitudinal view
eGFR/creatinine curves with staging bands, dry-weight trends, access history (fistula/catheter timeline), transplant-workup status.
### Scheme & package billing
PM-JAY and state-scheme dialysis packages auto-applied per session; consumables auto-consumed; monthly claim bundles generated.
### AV access surveillance
Access exam checklists each session; flow problems flag the vascular referral loop.
