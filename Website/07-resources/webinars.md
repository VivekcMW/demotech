# Webinars

## Recurring series

1. **The Friday Walkthrough** — a 30-minute live demo, rotating focus weekly (this week the TPA desk, next week the ICU dashboard); real product, live questions, no slides
2. **Specialty Spotlights** — monthly: one specialty's full workflow, co-presented with a practising clinician from that specialty
3. **Compliance Clinics** — quarterly expert sessions: ABDM updates, NABH preparation, DPDP obligations — with guest assessors and consultants
4. **Owner's Hour** — quarterly, for promoters and CEOs: benchmarks from anonymised platform data (receivable days, occupancy patterns, leakage norms)

## Page mechanics

Upcoming sessions with registration; the full replay library, searchable by topic and specialty; every replay paired with its related guide and module page. Registration asks for facility type and size, so follow-up is relevant rather than generic.
