## The problems we built around
- **Cataract is a throughput business:** Counseling-to-surgery conversion and OT turnover decide unit economics.
- **Refraction data is the clinical currency:** Serial vision and refraction must compare instantly across visits.
- **Camps feed the hospital:** Outreach screening data rarely connects to the surgical pipeline.

## How ophthalmology runs on AarogyaEHR
### Cataract pipeline
Diagnosis → counseling (IOL options & pricing) → biometry capture → surgery scheduling → day-1/week-1/month-1 outcome tracking, with conversion analytics per counselor.
### Refraction & vision records
Structured VA/refraction grids with prior-visit comparison; glass prescription prints; optical-shop order linkage.
### IOL & consumable inventory
Power-wise IOL stock, barcode pick at surgery, auto-billing, expiry control.
### Camp module
Offline-tolerant screening lists from outreach camps; referred patients arrive pre-registered with screening data attached.
### Specialty clinics
Glaucoma (IOP trending, field-test comparisons), retina (injection cycles via Daycare, diabetic-retinopathy grading loop with Diabetology).
