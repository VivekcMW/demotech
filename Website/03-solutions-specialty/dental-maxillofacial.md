## The problems we built around
- **Dental records are spatial:** Findings belong on a tooth chart, not in paragraphs.
- **Treatment is multi-visit and pre-quoted:** Plans, estimates, and stage-wise payments need structure patients can trust.
- **Chains need standardization:** Multi-clinic dental brands live or die on protocol and pricing consistency.

## How dental runs on AarogyaEHR
### Graphical tooth charting
FDI-notation interactive charts for findings and completed work; pediatric/mixed dentition support; perio charting grids.
### Treatment planning & estimates
Multi-stage plans with itemized estimates, alternative-option quotes, stage-wise billing and consent.
### Chair & appointment management
Chair-wise scheduling with procedure-duration intelligence, recall programs (scaling/ortho adjustments), no-show management.
### Imaging integration
IOPA/OPG/CBCT attachment to tooth-level findings; pre/post comparisons.
### OMFS & implant registry
Maxillofacial trauma and orthognathic documentation; implant placements logged with fixture details and recall schedules.
