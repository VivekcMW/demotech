## Who this is for

Single-specialty networks — eye-care chains, IVF networks, dental and derma/aesthetics chains, ortho and ENT day-surgery groups. Your business model is standardisation: the same protocol, counselling script, and pricing in centre 1 and centre 31. Your software has to enforce that, not hope for it.

---

## The problems we solve at this scale

**Protocol drift across centres.** When each centre documents differently, clinical quality and audit both suffer. {Product} pushes one template library, one order-set library, and one counselling workflow to every centre — versioned centrally, adopted instantly.

**The counsellor conversion gap.** In eye, IVF, dental, and aesthetics, the counsellor's funnel — advised → counselled → converted — is the revenue engine. {Product} tracks it natively: package quotes, EMI/financing notes, follow-up tasks, and conversion analytics per counsellor per centre.

**Camp and outreach chaos.** Eye and dental chains feed centres through camps and tie-ups. Camp registration on tablets, on-the-spot screening records, and automatic referral of surgical conversions into the home centre's schedule.

**Head office flying blind.** Centre-wise P&L, surgeon productivity, package profitability, and cancellation reasons — live, not in next month's deck.
